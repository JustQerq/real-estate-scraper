U oglasima za nekretnine sprat se najčešće skraćuje jednostavno kao sp ili s (npr. I sprat ili I s).
U opisima stanova često ćete naići i na sledeće standardne oznake:

* PR – Prizemlje
* VPR – Visoko prizemlje
* SUTR – Suteren
* NP – Niski prizemlje
* PK – Potkrovlje
* NPR – Niski prizemlje / Polusuteren
* I, II, III... – Broj sprata (rimskim brojevima)

Primer: Oznaka VPR/4 znači da se stan nalazi na visokom prizemlju u zgradi koja ima ukupno 4 sprata.

====================================================================================================

In real estate ads the floor is usually abbreviated simply as sp or s (eg I sprat or I s).
In apartment descriptions, you will often come across the following standard labels:

* PR – Ground floor
* VPR - High ground floor
* SUTR – Basement
* NP – Low ground floor
* PK - Attic
* NPR – Low ground floor / Semi-basement
* I, II, III... - Floor number (in Roman numerals)

Example: The designation VPR/4 means that the apartment is located on the upper ground floor of a building with a total of 4 floors.
